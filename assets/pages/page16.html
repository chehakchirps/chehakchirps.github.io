Many of us also associate certain songs to certain important moments in our life or certain people.

Maybe you remember the music playing while on your first date, or maybe a song your mom used to wake you up to, everyday at home. I’m sure you’ve known people who connect over similar tastes of music and others who had opposite music tastes but added that extra flavor in each other’s lives to complete each other.

Life is a musical montage. All we need to do is string it in tune.

They say music transcends all boundaries of caste, race, gender, religion.
